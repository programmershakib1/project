Donate for Child Labor in Bangladesh

Child labor is a serious social problem in Bangladesh. Thousands of children are working in various sectors of the country, especially in agriculture, construction, and factories. Many of them cannot go to school, thus being deprived of education. Child labor damages physical and mental health and hinders long-term social and economic development. Awareness against child labor is being raised through the efforts of the government and various organizations, but more effective steps are needed. It is everyone's responsibility to protect children's rights and ensure they have the opportunity to grow up in a safe, healthy environment. Come forward to help them.

Donate for Helpless People in Palestine

In Palestine, many people face profound challenges due to ongoing conflict and political instability. Daily life is marked by economic hardship, restricted access to basic services like healthcare and education, and the constant threat of violence. Families often struggle to secure food and safe shelter, while children grow up amid uncertainty and trauma. Despite their resilience and community spirit, the harsh realities of occupation and displacement leave many feeling helpless and in desperate need of support and advocacy for their rights and dignity.
